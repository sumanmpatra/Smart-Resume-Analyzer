Job Title: Software Engineer, University Graduate

Location: Bangalore / Hyderabad / Gurgaon, India

About the Job:
Join Google’s dynamic engineering team to build products used by billions. As a Software Engineer, you’ll design, develop, test, deploy, and improve software solutions. We look for engineers who bring fresh ideas and are ready to tackle challenging technical problems.

Responsibilities:
- Write clean, maintainable, and efficient code.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams on large-scale projects.
- Participate in code reviews and mentor junior engineers.
- Troubleshoot and debug technical issues across the stack.
- Contribute to engineering standards and best practices.

Minimum Qualifications:
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, related technical field, or equivalent practical experience.
- Strong programming skills in one or more of: Python, Java, C++, Go.
- Good understanding of data structures and algorithms.
- Experience with distributed systems or backend technologies.

Preferred Qualifications:
- Master's degree or PhD in Computer Science or related field.
- Hands-on experience with cloud platforms like Google Cloud, AWS, or Azure.
- Knowledge of machine learning fundamentals or frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch.
- Contributions to open-source projects.
- Strong communication and teamwork skills.

Skills & Tools:
- Python, Java, C++

- System Design
- Cloud Computing (GCP, AWS)
